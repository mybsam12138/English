# English Grammar: Common Mistakes Summary

## 1. Subject-Verb Agreement

### ❌ Wrong:

* He **go** to work every day

### ✅ Correct:

* He **goes** to work every day

👉 Rule:

* Singular subject → singular verb
* Plural subject → plural verb

---

## 2. Tense Consistency

### ❌ Wrong:

* Yesterday I **go** to the office

### ✅ Correct:

* Yesterday I **went** to the office

👉 Rule:

* Keep tense consistent with time expressions

---

## 3. Missing Articles (a / an / the)

### ❌ Wrong:

* I bought **car**

### ✅ Correct:

* I bought **a car**

👉 Rule:

* Use **a/an** for general
* Use **the** for specific

---

## 4. Incorrect Prepositions

### ❌ Wrong:

* I am good **in** English

### ✅ Correct:

* I am good **at** English

👉 Common examples:

* good at
* interested in
* responsible for

---

## 5. Run-on Sentences

### ❌ Wrong:

* I was tired I went home

### ✅ Correct:

* I was tired, so I went home
* I went home because I was tired

👉 Rule:

* Use conjunctions or punctuation

---

## 6. Sentence Fragments

### ❌ Wrong:

* Because I was tired

### ✅ Correct:

* I stayed home because I was tired

👉 Rule:

* A sentence must have a complete idea

---

## 7. Wrong Word Form

### ❌ Wrong:

* This is very **interest**

### ✅ Correct:

* This is very **interesting**

👉 Rule:

* Use correct adjective/adverb form

---

## 8. Confusing Similar Words

### ❌ Examples:

* affect vs effect
* then vs than
* your vs you’re

👉 Tip:

* Learn common confusing pairs

---

## 9. Overusing Simple Sentences

### ❌ Weak:

* I worked hard. I was tired. I went home.

### ✅ Better:

* I went home because I was tired after working hard

👉 Use clauses to improve fluency

---

## 10. Direct Translation from Chinese

### ❌ Wrong:

* Very like this

### ✅ Correct:

* I like this very much

👉 Rule:

* Think in English, not translate word-by-word

---

## 11. Missing Plural Forms

### ❌ Wrong:

* Many **student**

### ✅ Correct:

* Many **students**

---

## 12. Incorrect Use of “There is / There are”

### ❌ Wrong:

* There is many problems

### ✅ Correct:

* There are many problems

---

## 13. Using “Very” Too Much

### ❌ Weak:

* very big, very good, very important

### ✅ Better:

* huge, excellent, critical

---

## 14. Incorrect Word Order

### ❌ Wrong:

* I very like this

### ✅ Correct:

* I really like this

---

## 15. Speaking Tip (Important for You)

👉 Avoid:

* stopping too much
* translating in your head

👉 Do:

* use simple sentences
* use clauses (because, if, when)

---

## 16. One-Sentence Summary

> Common English mistakes include subject-verb agreement, tense errors, missing articles, incorrect prepositions, and direct translation from native language.
