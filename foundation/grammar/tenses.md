# English Tenses (Practical Summary)

## 1. Overview

English has many tenses, but in real usage, most communication relies on a few core ones.

👉 Focus on:
- Present Simple
- Past Simple
- Present Perfect
- Future (basic forms)

---

## 2. Present Simple

### Usage
- Facts
- Habits
- General truths

### Structure
Subject + base verb

### Examples
- I work as a developer
- The system handles requests
- We use Redis for caching

---

## 3. Present Continuous

### Usage
- Actions happening now
- Temporary situations

### Structure
Subject + am/is/are + verb-ing

### Examples
- I am working on a new feature
- We are fixing a bug
- The system is processing data

---

## 4. Past Simple

### Usage
- Completed actions in the past

### Structure
Subject + past verb

### Examples
- I implemented this feature
- We fixed the issue
- The system crashed yesterday

---

## 5. Past Continuous

### Usage
- Action in progress in the past

### Structure
Subject + was/were + verb-ing

### Examples
- I was working when the issue happened
- We were testing the system

---

## 6. Present Perfect

### Usage
- Experience
- Result affecting now

### Structure
Subject + have/has + past participle

### Examples
- I have worked on several projects
- We have improved performance
- The system has been updated

---

## 7. Past Perfect

### Usage
- Action completed before another past action

### Structure
Subject + had + past participle

### Examples
- I had finished the task before the meeting
- The system had already failed

---

## 8. Future (Basic Forms)

### 1. Will
Used for decisions, predictions

- I will implement this feature
- It will improve performance

### 2. Be going to
Used for plans

- I am going to refactor the code
- We are going to deploy the system

---

## 9. Most Important Tenses (Priority)

👉 In real life, these cover most situations:

1. Present Simple
2. Past Simple
3. Present Perfect

---

## 10. Practical Patterns

Instead of memorizing rules, use patterns:

- I use X to do Y
- We implemented X to solve Y
- This improves X
- One challenge was X
- We solved it by doing X

---

## 11. Common Mistakes

❌ I very like this  
✅ I really like this

❌ I have went there  
✅ I have gone there

❌ He suggest me  
✅ He suggested that I...

---

## 12. Key Advice

- Don’t try to master all tenses at once
- Focus on using them in real sentences
- Practice speaking and writing with them

👉 Grammar is a tool, not the goal
